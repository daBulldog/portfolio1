I’m a senior at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, majoring in Mechanical Engineering and Design, Innovation, & Society, with a minor in Studio Arts. My focus is in merging human-centered design and engineering. I’ve led projects from designing an ornithopter for crop monitoring to developing a mechanism for accessibility in sneakers and jewelry, combining technical skills with creative problem-solving.

Proficient in CAD, 3D modeling, rapid prototyping, and electronics, I also contribute to Instructables.com and have taught 500,000+ viewers through 3D modeling tutorials on YouTube. I thrive in collaborative environments, bringing curiosity and innovation to every project.
